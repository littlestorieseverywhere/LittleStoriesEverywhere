Hi, I'm Massi — event designer and creator behind Little Stories Everywhere. I've spent years designing moments for the people I love: weddings, proposals, baby showers, birthday celebrations. Every single one photographed beautifully, felt personal, and left people wanting to stay just a little longer.

What makes me different isn't just the planning. It's the custom handmade backdrops and decor I create from scratch for every event — pieces you won't find anywhere else, built specifically for you. Nobody else at this level is doing that.

Based in Greater Vancouver and available throughout the Sea-to-Sky Corridor and Fraser Valley. If you're ready to build something beautiful together, let's talk.
