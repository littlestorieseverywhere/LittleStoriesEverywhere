The woman
behind the story

Little Stories Everywhere was born from a belief that every event deserves to feel like a world — not just a party. A world with a mood, a colour, a feeling that stays with you long after the candles burn out.

Based in Vancouver, BC, I am an event planner and prop designer who handcrafts every piece myself. From giant foam bow arches to paper flower walls, rattan candelabras to draped fabric ceilings — everything you see at a Little Stories event was made with intention and care.

What makes Little Stories different is simple: I don't just plan events. I build atmospheres. I tell stories through décor, light, texture, and detail — and I don't stop until it gives you that feeling. You know the one. The moment you walk in and just stop.

✦
Handcrafted everything

Every prop is made by hand. No mass-produced backdrops, no generic rental pieces. When you hire Little Stories, you get something that exists nowhere else in Vancouver.

Whimsical at heart

The aesthetic is maximalist, playful, and unapologetically beautiful. We don't do basic. We do the kind of events that make people pull out their phones the moment they walk in.

Every event we create is specific to you — your colours, your feeling, your moment. We bring the expertise. You bring the vision. Together we build something unforgettable.
