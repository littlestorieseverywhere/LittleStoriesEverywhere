Little Stories Everywhere — Vancouver

Little Stories Everywhere was born from a simple belief: every event deserves to feel like a world of its own. Not just a party. A world with a mood, a colour palette, a feeling that lingers long after the candles burn out.

I am a Vancouver-based event planner and prop designer who handcrafts every piece myself. From giant foam bow arches to paper flower walls, rattan candelabras to draped fabric ceilings — everything you see at a Little Stories event was made with intention.

What makes Little Stories different is simple: I don't just plan events. I build atmospheres. I tell stories through décor, light, texture, and detail — and I don't stop until it gives you that feeling. You know the one. The moment you walk in and just stop.

What we stand for
01
Handcrafted everything

No mass-produced pieces. No generic rentals. Every prop is made from scratch, specifically for your event. When you hire Little Stories, you get something that exists nowhere else in Vancouver.

02
Whimsical at heart

The aesthetic is maximalist, playful, and unapologetically beautiful. We don't do basic. We do the kind of events that make people pull out their phones the moment they walk in.

03
Your story, always

Every event we create is specific to you — your colours, your feeling, your moment. We bring the expertise. You bring the vision. Together we build something unforgettable.
